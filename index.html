Presentation


I am an Inria researcher (CR) working with McTAO team at Centre Inria d'Université Côte d'Azur and Laboratoire J.A. Dieudonné of Université Côte d'Azur.

My topics of interest lie in control theory, sub-Riemannian geometry and their interactions.

These include analysis in sub-Riemannian manifolds, optimal control, observers and other estimation problems. Interactions with mathematical neuroscience.
